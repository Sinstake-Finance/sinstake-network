About

Sinstake Finance is Decentralized Finance Protocol (DEFI) Operating on a Binance Smart Chain which Provides a Staking Platform to it’s Players by Depositing BEP-20 Token Assets and Let Them Earn Active and Passive Dividend Earnings Daily. Sustainable Yield is being generated by Community Activities, Prize Pool Allocations and from Upcoming Pools. With the Power of Transparent Organization and Independent Audition of our Pools, Stored Earnings and Assets will be Resistant to Rug-Pulls and similar Dangers.
