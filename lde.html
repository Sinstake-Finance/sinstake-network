ELEPHANT Mini Guide

ELEPHANT.MONEY's token is a permissionless system for economic inclusion and helps its community accumulate wealth through 10% fees/burns on every transaction.

Distribution

A total of one quadrillion ELEPHANT tokens 49% tokens sent to graveyard, 490T tokens (burn address with auto burn)
1% for development/marketing
25% to contract owned locked liquidity
25% to community via liquidity drive

 

Auto Balancing Burn

In most reflect token platforms the burn address is passive and grows over time eating up all the profits. In ELEPHANT, the graveyard is a smart contract that rebalances ownership of the supply to 50% daily as needed.

 

Fees

10% of every transaction for ELEPHANT.MONEY goes to existing tokenn holders and locked liquidity, split equally 5%. The burn address is treated as just another holder.

 

Lifetime Rewards

ELEPHANT.MONEY creates passive income through volatility. Participants enjoy consistent rewards that are resistant to market down cycles.
